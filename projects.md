## SETA Scenario Generator

### Concept

A tool that takes a security policy or control requirement and generates scenario-based security awareness questions, answers, and rationales.

### Problem It Addresses

Many security awareness programmes are generic. Staff may complete training without understanding how security policies apply to their day-to-day work.

### Possible Features

- Upload or paste policy text
- Generate role-based scenarios
- Generate multiple-choice questions
- Provide answer rationales
- Map questions to policy clauses or control themes
- Produce microlearning content for newsletters or staff briefings

### Skills Demonstrated

- Security awareness design
- Policy interpretation
- Human risk reduction
- AI-assisted content generation
- Practical GRC communication

## ISO/IEC 27001 ISMS Setup Guide

### Concept

A guided checklist that helps organizations understand what ISMS materials and governance processes they may need based on size, sector, regulatory exposure, and maturity.

### Problem It Addresses

Small and mid-sized organizations often struggle to understand what an ISMS actually requires beyond documentation.

### Possible Features

- Organization profile questions
- ISMS document checklist
- Risk assessment workflow
- Statement of Applicability guidance
- Internal audit readiness checklist
- Management review preparation notes

### Skills Demonstrated

- ISO/IEC 27001 understanding
- ISMS design
- Governance planning
- Risk treatment thinking
- Practical implementation support

## Vendor Risk Dashboard

### Concept

A lightweight dashboard for tracking vendor security assessments, risk ratings, due dates, review status, and outstanding remediation actions.

### Problem It Addresses

Vendor security reviews can become inconsistent if assessment depth, evidence, risk rating, and follow-up are not tracked in a structured way.

### Possible Features

- Vendor inventory
- Risk classification
- Data sensitivity indicator
- Service criticality rating
- Assessment status
- Contract/SLA review status
- Corrective action tracking
- Review due dates

### Skills Demonstrated

- Third-party risk management
- Risk scoring
- Dashboard design
- Control assurance tracking
- Management reporting

## BIA Cheatsheet

### Concept

A simplified business impact analysis reference that helps non-technical stakeholders understand impact categories, recovery expectations, and dependency mapping.

### Problem It Addresses

BIA exercises often fail when business users do not understand impact types, recovery priorities, or how to quantify business consequences.

### Possible Features

- Impact category examples
- Recovery time objective explanation
- Recovery point objective explanation
- Dependency mapping guide
- Critical process questionnaire
- Example BIA scoring model

### Skills Demonstrated

- Business continuity planning
- Stakeholder communication
- Risk analysis
- Simplification of technical concepts

## Agreement Assessment Bot

### Concept

An AI-assisted tool that compares a vendor agreement, SLA, or service terms against an internal information security clause checklist.

### Problem It Addresses

Vendor agreements may omit important security, data protection, incident notification, availability, audit, or exit clauses. Manual review can be inconsistent and time-consuming.

### Possible Features

- Upload or paste contract/SLA text
- Compare against internal security clause checklist
- Flag missing or weak clauses
- Suggest mitigation controls if contract change is not possible
- Produce a risk summary for review
- Map gaps to risk owners such as Legal, IT, Operations, or Business

### Skills Demonstrated

- Contract risk review
- Vendor assurance
- AI-assisted analysis
- Security clause mapping
- Risk-based decision support

## Cyber Maturity Heatmap

### Concept

A dashboard or template that maps current cybersecurity controls against a maturity framework such as CIS Controls, NIST CSF, or an internal maturity model.

### Problem It Addresses

Management often needs a simple view of cybersecurity maturity, gaps, planned improvements, and residual risk.

### Possible Features

- Control domain heatmap
- Current control/evidence field
- Planned roadmap field
- Gap to validate field
- Maturity status labels
- Management summary view

### Skills Demonstrated

- Cyber maturity assessment
- Security roadmap planning
- Control gap analysis
- Executive reporting
