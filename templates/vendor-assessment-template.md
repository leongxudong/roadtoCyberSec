## Suggested Assessment Depth

| Risk Level | Typical Criteria | Assessment Approach |
|---|---|---|
| Low | No sensitive data, no system access, low operational dependency | Basic due diligence and business owner confirmation |
| Medium | Some data exposure, moderate dependency, or limited system integration | Standard vendor questionnaire and contract/SLA review |
| High | Sensitive data, privileged access, critical service, or regulatory exposure | Full security assessment, evidence review, contract review, and risk acceptance where required |
