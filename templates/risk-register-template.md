# Risk Register Template

This is a lightweight risk register template for cybersecurity, IT governance, compliance, and operational risk tracking.

## Risk Register

| Risk ID | Risk Statement | Asset / Process | Threat / Issue | Impact | Likelihood | Risk Rating | Existing Controls | Treatment Plan | Risk Owner | Target Date | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| R-001 | If [event] occurs due to [cause], then [impact] may result. |  |  | High / Medium / Low | High / Medium / Low | High / Medium / Low |  | Accept / Mitigate / Transfer / Avoid |  |  | Open / In Progress / Closed |

## Risk Statement Format

Use this structure:

> If [threat/event] occurs due to [vulnerability/cause], then [business/security impact] may result.

Example:

> If a critical SaaS vendor suffers prolonged service outage due to weak availability commitments, then business operations may be disrupted because there is no defined recovery workaround.

## Rating Guide

### Impact

| Rating | Description |
|---|---|
| High | Significant operational, regulatory, financial, reputational, or safety impact |
| Medium | Noticeable impact requiring management attention or coordinated remediation |
| Low | Limited impact manageable within normal operations |

### Likelihood

| Rating | Description |
|---|---|
| High | Expected to occur or has occurred repeatedly |
| Medium | Possible under realistic conditions |
| Low | Unlikely but plausible |

## Treatment Options

| Treatment | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Accept | Business accepts the residual risk |
| Mitigate | Implement controls to reduce impact or likelihood |
| Transfer | Shift part of the risk through contract, insurance, or outsourcing |
| Avoid | Stop the activity or remove the risk source |

## Notes

Risk entries should be specific, evidence-based, assigned to an owner, and reviewed periodically.
